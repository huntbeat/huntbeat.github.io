What is Taiga?

Taiga is a dockless bike-sharing service for communities, built by Swarthmore students. Other bike-share services rely on creating whole bikes and systems themselves; we reshape existing bikes for our service to make bike-sharing more affordable and diverse. We aim to change how people move on every university campus, city, and town across the nation.

How does it work?

Download our app on the App Store or
on Google Play to get started!

1. Share your bike!

If your bike's fit to ride, grab a lock from us and you're ready to share. Done! Earn 25 cents for every mile that your bike travels. Don't have a bike? Adopt available bikes with points!

2. Start riding!

Start and end your rides anywhere, any time. Rides start at $0.50 for every half-hour.

3. Earn points!

Earn points when you park near parking hubs or when your bikes help move others around. Redeem points for adopting bikes, free rides, and awesome goodies!
